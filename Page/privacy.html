隐私政策 Privacy Policy

更新日期：2026 年 3 月 Last updated: March 2026

SooFlow（以下简称"我们"）非常重视您的隐私，并致力于保护您的个人信息。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和存储您在使用本应用时的信息。

SooFlow ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use our application.

01

我们收集的信息 Information We Collect

目前，SooFlow 不需要用户注册账户。

我们可能收集以下信息：

  • 存储在您设备本地的使用数据（如拉伸记录、偏好设置、进度信息等）
  • 基础设备信息（用于应用功能与优化）

在未来版本中，我们可能会收集：

  • 通过 Apple 或第三方服务获取的订阅与购买信息
  • 用于改进产品体验的使用行为数据（如分析数据）

Currently, SooFlow does not require account registration.

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Usage data stored locally on your device (e.g., completed routines, preferences, progress data)
  • Basic device information (for app functionality and optimization)

In future versions, we may collect:

  • Subscription and purchase information via Apple or third-party services
  • Analytics data to improve product experience

02

信息的使用方式 How We Use Information

我们使用信息用于：

  • 提供和优化应用核心功能
  • 生成个性化拉伸流程与推荐
  • 提升用户体验和性能表现

We use information to:

  • Provide and improve core app functionality
  • Generate personalized routines and recommendations
  • Enhance user experience and app performance

03

数据存储方式 Data Storage

目前，所有用户数据均存储在您的本地设备中。

我们不会将您的个人数据上传至服务器或进行远程存储。

未来若引入云同步等功能，我们将更新本隐私政策。

All user data is currently stored locally on your device.

We do not upload or store your personal data on external servers at this stage.

Future versions may introduce cloud-based features (such as syncing), in which case this policy will be updated accordingly.

这些服务可能会根据其隐私政策收集有限信息。

SooFlow may use third-party services, including:

  • Apple (in-app purchases)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • PostHog (analytics tools)

These services may collect limited information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

05

信息共享 Data Sharing

我们不会出售、出租或交易您的个人信息。

我们不会将您的数据用于广告追踪或商业营销。

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

06

用户权利 User Rights

由于当前未使用账户系统，您的数据完全由您本地控制。

您可以通过以下方式删除数据：

  • 清除应用数据

Since SooFlow currently does not use accounts, your data is controlled locally.

You may delete your data at any time by:

  • Clearing app data

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since data is stored locally, security depends partly on your device's security settings.
