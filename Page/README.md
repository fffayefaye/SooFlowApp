## 简体中文

**舒服喽**是一款专为久坐上班族、手机重度用户和睡前放松需求设计的 iOS 拉伸 App。每次 3–10 分钟，无需任何器械，随时随地缓解颈肩酸痛、腰背紧绷和腹胀不适。

**核心场景**
- 🧘 颈肩拉伸 — 缓解长时间低头、伏案带来的颈椎压力
- 🫁 腹胀缓解 — 饭后或久坐引起的腹胀，专项舒缓动作
- 💼 久坐放松 — 专为上班族设计，工位上即可完成
- 🌙 睡前放松 — 帮助身体从紧绷状态平静下来，改善睡眠质量

**功能亮点**
- 每日提醒，养成拉伸好习惯
- 自动同步 Apple 健康（运动记录）
- 三语界面：简体中文 / 繁體中文 / English
- 小熊猫吉祥物 Sooli 全程陪伴引导

## English

**SooFlow** is an iOS stretching app built for desk workers, heavy phone users, and anyone who needs a quick reset. Each session takes just 3–10 minutes with zero equipment — follow red panda Sooli through guided stretches and feel the relief instantly.

**Scenes**
- 🧘 Neck & Shoulder — relieve tension from long hours hunched over screens
- 🫁 Bloating Relief — gentle movements to ease post-meal or sedentary bloating
- 💼 Desk Worker — designed to be done right at your workstation
- 🌙 Before Sleep — wind down your body and improve sleep quality

**Features**
- Daily reminders to build a lasting stretch habit
- Automatic Apple Health sync (workout records)
- Three languages: 简体中文 / 繁體中文 / English
- Red panda mascot Sooli guides every session
